Privacy Policy

QScanner — QR & Barcode

Last Updated: July 2026  ·  Effective Date: July 2026

QScanner ("the App") is developed and published by Mevinu Abeysinghe ("Developer", "we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how QScanner handles information when you use the application on Android or iOS devices.

QScanner is designed as a fast, reliable QR code and barcode scanner. The App does not operate any cloud service, backend server, or account system for storing your personal scanning history on our servers. Any scanning history is stored locally on your device.

By using QScanner, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

QScanner does not collect or transmit your scanning history to our servers. Limited device information may be collected automatically by third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob and Meta (Facebook) Audience Network.

The App may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Scanned QR codes and barcodes
  • App settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device and is never accessible to the Developer.

2. Local Data Storage

All scanned data saved within QScanner is stored locally on your device. The app uses industry-standard local storage mechanisms (such as SharedPreferences and local databases) to maintain your settings and scan history. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the App.

While local storage is used, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Users should ensure their device is protected with a secure lock screen.

3. Device Permissions

QScanner may request access to certain device capabilities solely for the following purposes:

  • Camera: Required to scan QR codes and barcodes in real-time. The camera feed is processed entirely on-device. The App does not capture, store, or transmit photos or video to any external server or to the Developer.
  • Storage / Photos: Required if you choose to scan codes from images stored on your device.

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings, though this will limit the core functionality of the App.

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services

QScanner uses the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob) and Meta (Facebook) Audience Network to display advertisements. These third-party services may collect certain device data to serve personalized ads and analyze ad performance.

Data Collected by Advertising Partners

These partners may automatically collect the following information for the purpose of ad delivery, frequency capping, fraud prevention, and performance measurement:

  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address (used to infer approximate geographic location for ad targeting)
  • Device information (device model, OS version, screen resolution)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, view duration)
  • App activity related to ad interactions

This data is collected and processed by these companies in accordance with their policies. QScanner does not access, process, or share your local scan data with any advertising provider.

For full details on how they handle this data, please refer to their respective Privacy Policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Meta (Facebook) Privacy Policy: https://www.facebook.com/about/privacy/

Your Advertising Choices

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time:

  • Android: Settings › Google › Ads › Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings › Privacy & Security › Tracking › disable tracking for QScanner
  • You may also reset your Advertising ID at any time from your device settings

5. No Analytics or Tracking by the Developer

Aside from the third-party advertising networks mentioned in Section 4, QScanner does not include any analytics, user behavior tracking, or crash reporting tools operated by or accessible to the Developer. Specifically, the App does not include:

  • User behavior or session analytics sent to our servers
  • Account creation or login systems
  • Cloud synchronization of any kind
  • Any telemetry or usage statistics sent to the Developer

6. Data Safety (Google Play)

In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety requirements, the following summarizes data practices for QScanner:

  • No personal data is collected or shared by the Developer
  • All scan data is stored locally on-device
  • Third-party ad networks (AdMob, Meta) may collect Advertising ID and device information for ads (see Section 4)
  • Users can delete all app data at any time (see Section 7)
  • The App does not collect data from children

7. User Control and Data Deletion

You have full control over all data stored by QScanner. You may delete all stored data at any time by:

  • Using the option to clear history within the App (if available)
  • Clearing the app's cache and data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the App — the operating system deletes all associated app data upon uninstallation

There is no account to delete, and no data exists on any server that requires deletion requests.

8. Children's Privacy

QScanner does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). The App does not intentionally target children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us using the details in Section 10. We will take prompt steps to address the issue.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App after any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. For significant changes, we will endeavor to notify users through an in-app notice or app update notes.
